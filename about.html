About Homer

Homer is a modern rental management app that makes renting simpler, smarter, and more transparent for both property owners and tenants.

What Homer Does

Homer is designed to help owners and tenants manage rental agreements, payments, and history — all in one place. Key features include:

  • 📄 Digital Agreements – Create, store, and access rental agreements securely.
  • 💳 Secure UPI Payments – Pay and receive rent directly through the app.
  • ⭐ Rental Score – Track payment discipline and build trust with a verified rental history.
  • 📜 Complete History – Keep all payment records and agreements in one place.

Why Choose Homer?

  • ✅ Simplifies the rental process for both parties
  • ✅ Enhances transparency and trust
  • ✅ Secure data storage with encryption
  • ✅ Easy-to-use modern interface
