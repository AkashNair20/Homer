About Homer

Renting made transparent—proof over promises.

Homer’s mission is to make renting transparent by turning on-time rent payments into proof of reliability—so renters get great homes faster and owners choose great tenants with evidence, not trial and error.

Our mission

Make renting fair, fast, and transparent for everyone involved by aligning incentives with verified history.

  • • Proof beats promises: your payment history speaks for you.
  • • Privacy by default: you control what’s shared and when.

Trust

Verified history replaces guesswork and bias for faster approvals.

Simplicity

UPI/Razorpay payments, automated receipts, and clean dashboards.

Privacy

Share your score—nothing more—unless you choose to.
