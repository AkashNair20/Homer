Frequently asked questions

Is Homer a wallet?

No. Homer uses trusted payment rails (e.g., Razorpay) to process rent and records your payment history and score.

What is Homer Score?

A weighted score of on-time vs late vs missed rent payments. Sharing it helps owners quickly trust your profile.

Are my payments secure?

Yes. We use PCI-compliant processors and never store sensitive card/UPI credentials on our servers.

Can owners see my data?

Only what you choose to share—like your score, payment streak, and reviews.
