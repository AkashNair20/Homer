Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28/09/2025   |   Last Updated: 28/09/2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number (for login and identification)
  • Name, flat details, and PIN code
  • Optional UPI ID for rent tracking
  • Device data and crash analytics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify your identity and allow login
  • Manage and display rental agreements and rental scores
  • Maintain rental history and records
  • Improve our services and app performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., Google Firebase for data storage)
  • Third-party tools (e.g., Razorpay if you choose to track rent payments)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Third-Party Services

Homer uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

6. User Rights

You may:

7. Children's Privacy

Homer is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after updates implies acceptance of the revised terms.

9. Consent

By using Homer, you agree to this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your information as described.

10. Refund Policy

If a payment is charged in error or otherwise processed incorrectly within Homer, the full amount will be refunded to the renter. Refunds are initiated after a basic review to confirm the issue. Timing for funds to appear back in your account may vary based on your bank or payment partner. For assistance, please reach us at support@homer.co.in.
